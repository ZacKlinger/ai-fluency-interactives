Four behaviors. Two loops.

Start with what you do every day — then see what surrounds it.

Quality Focus
Description + Discernment
→ An iterative cycle to ensure a high-quality product.

You craft a description for what you need, evaluate what AI returns with Discernment, and refine — repeating the loop until the result genuinely serves your students.

Ethical Focus
Delegation + Diligence
→ A protective agency for strong ethical standards.

Before and after the quality loop, these behaviors ask the harder questions: should AI be here at all, and are you fully accountable for what it produces?
