In Review
Part One — Designing with AI

Tools teachers built. Problems teachers named.

We’re going to take a moment to tinker. You’ll get the chance to explore some tools other educators have already built — real projects made by teachers and school leaders through TFA’s AI hackathon program over the last two years. Take note of what problems they chose to solve. That’s often the hardest part.

Tool Name
Description
Problem to be solved
Your Future Career
An app that helps students think through their interests in order to tie those interests to a potential future career.
Their school had a goal to create more career connected learning for high school students but also only had one counselor for the school.
Fun Friday Planner
An app that helps make a plan for Fridays because it is our lowest attendance day. Teachers, Administrators and Parents can use this to plan and build their own fun Fridays.
Fridays were the lowest attended day of the week at their school. AI helps them brainstorm ideas that they build and run for students.
Lyrics to Learning
This chatbot helps teachers create engaging language arts lessons using hip-hop lyrics. Activities cover rhyming, vocabulary, storytelling, and more.
This teacher wanted to find a way to use their passion to create a more engaging ELA classroom. They used AI to infuse their lesson planning with their passion for hip hop.
